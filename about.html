ABOUT

Qowiy Prosthetic was Founded in 2037 in Indonesia with The Motto of "Empathy in Motion, Crafting Prosthetic Solutions", we always choose the Best Technologies. At Arm Prosthetic Corp, we are dedicated to transforming the lives of individuals with limb loss or limb difference. Our company is at the forefront of developing cutting-edge arm prosthetics that redefine mobility, independence, and quality of life.

At Prosthetic Corporation, research and development form the backbone of their operations. The company invests heavily in scientific research, collaborating with leading experts in biomechanics, materials science, and medical technology to push the boundaries of what is possible in the field of prosthetics. This commitment to innovation has resulted in numerous breakthroughs, including advancements in sensory feedback systems, neural interfaces, and bionic limbs.

Vision: The vision of Prosthetic Corporation is to be the global leader in prosthetic innovation, setting the standard for excellence in the field and revolutionizing the lives of individuals with limb loss. We envision a world where every person, regardless of their physical condition, has access to advanced, affordable, and customized prosthetic solutions that empower them to live life to the fullest.

Mission:

1. Innovation: We are dedicated to pushing the boundaries of prosthetic technology through relentless research and development. By collaborating with leading experts and embracing emerging technologies, we strive to develop groundbreaking solutions that go beyond mere replacement limbs, incorporating advanced features such as sensory feedback, neural interfaces, and bionic capabilities.

2. Empowerment: We believe in empowering individuals with limb loss to regain their independence, confidence, and dignity. Through our personalized approach, we work closely with patients, prosthetists, and healthcare professionals to deliver custom solutions that cater to each person's unique needs, anatomy, and functional goals. We provide comprehensive support throughout the entire prosthetic journey, including education, training, and rehabilitation services.

3. Accessibility: We are committed to making advanced prosthetic technology accessible to individuals from all walks of life. We strive to optimize our manufacturing processes, streamline costs, and work closely with insurance providers and healthcare organizations to ensure that our prosthetic solutions are affordable and covered by insurance policies. Our goal is to eliminate barriers and enable people to access the prosthetic care they deserve.

By pursuing our vision and executing our mission with unwavering dedication, Prosthetic Corporation aims to transform the landscape of prosthetic technology, enhance the lives of individuals with limb loss, and inspire a world where physical limitations do not hinder human potential.
